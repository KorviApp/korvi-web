How it works

From "what's for dinner?"
to done in three steps

Plan on Sunday, cook all week. It's that simple.

Plan your week

Browse recipes by cuisine, cook time, or diet. Tap to add them to any day. Your household sees the plan instantly.

Shop the list

Korvi generates a shopping list grouped by aisle. Take it to the store or share it — check items off together in real time.

Cook step by step

Open Cook Mode and follow along. Each step shows what you need and when to set a timer. Dinner is served.
